About Me

Hello! I’m Siddhi Chauhan.

I'm an Artist based in Mumbai, India with experience in delivering end-to-end custom Art pieces. I'm passionate about Art and believe in innovation and creating memorable interactive experiences.

As an Artist, I'm essentially interested in creating works that call for the participation of audience to the same extent as my involvement in it.

Art has been an intergal part of me. I spend most of my time creating art, and when I am not doing so, I'll probably be learning new things.I do not limit myself to one medium, style or concept, because it allows me to express different ideas.

If a viewer stops just for a moment to view and reflect on a piece I have created, then I have succeeded in my work.
